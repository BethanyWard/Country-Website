Illyria

Illyria, while not a country anymore, was apart of the northwestern Balkan Peninsula around 10th century BCE. In its prime,
Illyria was divided between three main tribes; the Paleo-Balkans, Thracians, and the Greeks. Like most of Europe, it was eventually
overtaken by the roman empire, where it actually grew to be fairly prosperous. The inhabitants of Illyria spoke Illyrian, (as hinted
by the name) although many scholars belive they spoke the also now extinct Venetic Language (an italic subgroup).
