Religion in Illyria

Similar to most ancient civilizations, Illyrians were polytheistic (meaning they worshiped more than one god).
Their gods mainly had roles in nature, and symbols found on coins, monuments and altars suggest they were apart
of the prehistoric cult of the sun. There doesn't seem to be one single prominent god like, and some deities
only appeared in specific regions of Illyria. They belived in supernatural powers mythological protectors, but
not much is known as far as specifics go.
