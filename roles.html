Illyrian Roles in Literature

Illyria is not a well known country outside of a few works; most prominently, it features in William Shakespeare's
Twelfth Night as the setting for the play. In the modernized movie version, She's The Man, Illyria becomes the name
of the high school. It's breifly mentioned in Part 2 of Henry VI. Illyria is also the fictional setting for John
Hawkes' novel, The Blood Oranges.
